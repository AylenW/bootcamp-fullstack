Aylen's recipe for the best cinnamon rolls

Ingredients

For the dought:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 cup bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

    • Instructions:

    1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs
    2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture
    3. Add in the flour and mix until combined into a duogh
    4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes
